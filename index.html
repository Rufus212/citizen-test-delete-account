Account Deletion Request

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This page explains how to delete your account and associated data for Citizen Test.

Delete Account In The App

You can request deletion directly inside the app:

  1. Open the app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion action.

What Is Deleted

  • Your account authentication record
  • Active sessions and local app profile data
  • Saved quiz progress and local test history stored in the app

What May Be Retained

Certain records may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or compliance reasons.

If You Cannot Access The App

If you cannot sign in, email us from your registered account email and request deletion: info@cernch.com

Please include your account email and the subject line: Account Deletion Request.

Processing Time

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days.
